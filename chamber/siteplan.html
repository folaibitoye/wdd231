Individual Website Project Site Plan

Site Name

Project Name: Adventure Rafting Hub

Reason for Name: Represents a website dedicated to rafting adventures, bookings, and local guides for outdoor enthusiasts.

Optional Domain: adventure-rafting.com

Site Purpose

The website serves as a comprehensive hub for rafting enthusiasts, providing trip details, booking options, safety guidelines, and local rafting resources.

Scenarios

  • Which rafting trips are suitable for beginners?
  • How can I book a rafting adventure and what are the costs?

Color Schema

Selected colors and usage on the website:

Headings and primary accents
Backgrounds and section accents
Buttons, highlights, or call-to-action

Typography

Heading Font: Roboto – used for all headings.

Body Font: Arial – used for paragraphs and general content.

Special Font: None currently; optional for buttons or calls-to-action.
